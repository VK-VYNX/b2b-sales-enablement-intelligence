# Category: Sales Enablement Content

Use this file for content strategy, governance, usability, and seller adoption.

## Expected Knowledge From This Category

- internal versus external enablement content
- content systems and governance
- content findability and usability
- alignment with buyer journey and methodology
- content effectiveness measurement

## Distillation Priorities

Prioritize extraction of:

- what makes content actionable in live selling
- how content should map to moments, roles, and stages
- governance and maintenance models
- measurement beyond raw usage counts

## Common Misread

Content volume is not content effectiveness.
