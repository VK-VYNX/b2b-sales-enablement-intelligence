# VYNX Principles

Use these principles as the sharpest expression of the VYNX operating philosophy.

1. No proposal without confidence.
2. Discovery must create movement.
3. Technical validation is a commercial event.
4. RFQ volume is not deal quality.
5. Plant reality outranks conference-room assumptions.
6. Service credibility wins industrial deals.
7. Distributor support must be earned, not assumed.
8. A quote is not a strategy.
9. Late-stage discounting often hides early-stage failure.
10. Commissioning risk must surface before proposal release.
11. Every strategic deal needs one operating owner.
12. Management cadence is part of the sales process.

## Usage Guidance

- Use these principles to frame executive summaries, workshops, and leadership discussions.
- Use them as decision filters when recommendations compete.
- Use them as naming anchors for scorecards, dashboards, and consulting deliverables.
