# VYNX Methodology Summary

The VYNX Industrial Deal Conversion System is a commercial operating method for industrial, engineering-led, and manufacturing revenue teams that generate quotes but under-convert complex opportunities.

It exists to fix the gap between technical activity and commercial conversion:

- too many RFQs are pursued without discipline
- plant visits collect information without creating momentum
- applications engineering is engaged inconsistently
- proposals describe solutions without reducing implementation risk

The system works by controlling the high-value deal path from qualification through plant discovery, technical validation, proposal readiness, commercial review, and service handoff. Each stage has:

- clear exit criteria
- named ownership
- required artifacts
- escalation rules
- management inspection
- CRM and CPQ controls

Distributor capability is tiered, technical and service resources are allocated intentionally, and leadership manages the system through conversion, progression, readiness, and execution-confidence signals.

What makes VYNX different from generic sales enablement is simple: this is not a training program, a content library, or a slide-driven methodology launch. It is an industrial revenue operating system built for:

- RFQs
- plant visits
- engineering validation
- commissioning risk
- distributor variance
- OEM and integrator dynamics
- service credibility
