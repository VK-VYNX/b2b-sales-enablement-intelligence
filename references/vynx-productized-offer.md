# VYNX Productized Offer

Use this file for consulting packaging, proposal writing, or positioning VYNX as the provider behind the methodology.

## Offer Name

VYNX Industrial Deal Conversion System

## Who It Is For

- industrial automation manufacturers
- OEM and engineered-solution suppliers
- manufacturers selling through distributors, integrators, and direct teams
- commercial leaders with strong quote volume but weak conversion on complex deals

## What Problems It Solves

- too many RFQs pursued with low win probability
- plant visits that do not create deal movement
- weak applications engineering handoffs and slow technical progression
- proposals that fail to reduce commissioning and implementation risk
- high distributor capability variance
- poor visibility into why strategic deals stall or die

## What Is Included

- VYNX audit framework
- maturity assessment
- 90-minute design workshop
- stage-gate operating model
- decision rules for AE, service, and escalation
- fillable deal-management templates
- distributor tiering model
- executive dashboard design
- 30-60-90 implementation plan

## Timeline

- Week 1: audit and evidence collection
- Week 2: diagnostic synthesis and workshop
- Week 3: operating-model design and template pack
- Week 4-6: pilot setup and management cadence launch
- Optional extension: 60-90 day implementation sprint

## Deliverables

- diagnostic readout
- maturity scorecard
- workshop outputs
- stage-gate scorecard
- proposal-readiness rubric
- plant-visit checklist
- technical-commercial handoff brief
- strategic deal review template
- distributor tiering scorecard
- leadership dashboard design
- pilot rollout plan

## Outcomes

- higher proposal-to-order conversion
- better use of technical resources
- stronger execution confidence in strategic deals
- clear distributor segmentation and support rules
- better management control of stage leakage and stalled deals

## Why VYNX Is Credible

- built for industrial and manufacturing sales realities, not generic B2B theory
- designed around RFQs, plant visits, applications engineering handoffs, commissioning risk, distributor variance, and service credibility
- connects commercial process to technical and operational execution
- produces implementation artifacts, not just recommendations
- suitable for audit, workshop, pilot, and operating rollout formats
