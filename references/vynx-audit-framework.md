# VYNX Audit Framework

Use this file for client-facing diagnostics and initial assessments.

## Objective

Assess why high-value industrial deals are being quoted but not converted, and identify the operating changes required across distributors, direct sales, applications engineering, service, and management.

## Assessment Dimensions

1. Opportunity qualification discipline
2. Plant visit and discovery quality
3. Technical validation and applications engineering handoff
4. Proposal and commercial readiness
5. Service and implementation credibility
6. Distributor capability and control
7. Management cadence and inspection
8. CRM and CPQ workflow control

## Evidence To Collect

- 20-30 recent high-value opportunities across won, lost, and stalled
- RFQs, proposals, plant-visit notes, applications engineering requests, review decks, and service handoffs
- CRM stage history, aging, loss reasons, and distributor attribution
- applications engineering turnaround data and proposal turnaround data
- interviews with channel leaders, regional sales managers, applications engineering leaders, service leaders, and RevOps
- 3-5 live strategic deal reviews
- distributor segment and performance breakdown
- examples of commissioning or service objections in late-stage deals

## Scoring Model

Use a 1-5 scale per dimension:

- 1: Reactive
- 2: Basic
- 3: Emerging
- 4: Controlled
- 5: Systemic

Suggested weighting:

- qualification discipline: 15%
- plant visit and discovery: 15%
- AE handoff and validation: 15%
- proposal and commercial readiness: 20%
- service credibility: 10%
- distributor control: 10%
- management cadence: 10%
- CRM and CPQ control: 5%

## Output Summary

- overall maturity score
- heatmap by dimension
- top 5 root causes
- top 5 intervention priorities
- stage leakage view from RFQ to proposal to order
- distributor capability segmentation
- 30-60-90 implementation recommendation
