# Category: Sales Methodology

Use this file for methodology choice, rollout, reinforcement, and adherence.

## Expected Knowledge From This Category

- methodology purpose and fit
- rollout and adoption
- integration into management systems
- behavior inspection
- methodology measurement

## Distillation Priorities

Prioritize extraction of:

- when a methodology is worth standardizing
- failure modes of methodology rollouts
- manager and leadership reinforcement mechanisms
- integration with onboarding, coaching, content, CRM, and pipeline reviews

## Common Misread

Methodology training alone does not create adoption.
